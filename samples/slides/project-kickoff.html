Background

Our platform, built in 2019, faces critical pain points.

6.2s

Mobile Load Time

Page load averages 6.2 seconds — far above the 2s target.

43%

Accessibility Score

WCAG compliance at 43% — well below the required threshold.

12

Inconsistent Areas

UX patterns vary across 12 product areas with no unified system.

2019

Legacy Codebase

Tech debt blocks rapid feature delivery and innovation.

---

Goals

Three measurable outcomes by end of 2026.

Performance — Time to Interactive 6.2s → < 2s
Consistency — Design System Coverage 0% → 100%
Accessibility — WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance 43% → 100%

Schedule

Q1

Discovery & Design System Foundations

Audit current platform, establish component library baseline.

Q2

Core Pages Rebuilt — Home, Search, Product

Migrate top-traffic pages to the new design system.

Q3

Account, Checkout & Dashboard Migration

Complete transactional flows and authenticated views.

Q4

Full Rollout & Legacy Sunset

Performance validation, final QA, and decommission of old platform.
