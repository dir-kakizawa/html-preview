Project NOVA

Web Platform Redesign Kickoff · Team Alpha · 2026 Q1

---

Background

Our current web platform was built in 2019. Over time, several critical issues have emerged:

  • Page load times average 6.2 seconds on mobile
  • Legacy codebase blocks rapid feature delivery
  • UX patterns are inconsistent across 12 product areas
  • Accessibility compliance is at 43% — well below target

Project NOVA is a full redesign to address all of the above.

---

Goals

  • Performance — reduce Time to Interactive to under 2 seconds on mobile
  • Consistency — ship a unified design system used across all product areas
  • Accessibility — achieve WCAG 2.1 AA compliance (target: 100%)
