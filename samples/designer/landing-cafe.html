Our Story

A Labor of Love Since 2018

Maison Brun began as a small corner roastery tucked away on Rue de la Paix. Founded by cousins Elise and Julien, it was born from a shared obsession: that exceptional coffee changes how people experience their day.

Today we source directly from family farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Japan's rare Kumamoto prefecture. Every bag is roasted in-house on our 1960s cast-iron drum, and every cup is pulled by baristas who trained for months before serving a single espresso.
